Friday Night

Making Cordage from Nettles and Thistles

After supper you are invited to join us in the meadow for dessert where Tania will teach us how to harvest thistles. We will then bring our thistles back to camp where Tania will give us a demo on preparing nettle and thistles for cordage.

Saturday Night

Meals

Please bring your own food for all meals.

For those interested Tania will be hosting potluck suppers Friday and Saturday night! Please bring your dish, labeled with ingredients.

Small electric coolers and crock pots can be plugged in at the shop.

Accommodations

Camping: $55 for the weekend. Tents and RVs welcome. No generators. No Dogs. Kids and partners are welcome.

Tents: Tania has a few extra tents we can use. Send us an email at basketinthegarden@gmail.com if you would like to reserve one.

Earth Loom

You are invited to collect grasses and flowers fromn the meadow and add them to the Earth Loom.

Open Weave

Take a break from classes and join us for an open weave, where anyone interested can add some willow to the collective creation. This year's theme is Forest Friend. Which forest friend? We will have to wait and see what emerges.
