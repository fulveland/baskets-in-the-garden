Friday Night

Making Cordage from Nettles and Thistles

After supper you are invited to join us in the meadow for dessert where Tania will teach us how to harvest thistles. We will then bring our thistles back to camp where Tania will give us a demo on preparing nettle and thistles for cordage.

Saturday Night

Dolly Weave

After Supper we will make our way back to the meadow for dessert! Only this time we will be collecting grasses, and flowers, to make a small doll.

Meals

Please bring your own food for all meals.

For those interested Tania will be hosting potluck suppers Friday and Saturday night! Please bring your dish, labeled with ingredients.

Small electric coolers and crock pots can be plugged in at the shop.

Accommodations

Camping: $55 for the weekend. Tents and RVs are welcome.

Please note: no generators and no dogs. Children and partners are welcome.

The $55 camping fee covers everyone sleeping in your tent or trailer.

For those who would like to come early or stay a little longer, early setup is available Thursday from 5–9 pm. On Monday, we ask that everyone depart by noon.

Need a Tent? Tania has a few extra tents available to borrow. If you would like to reserve one, please email us at: basketinthegarden@gmail.com
